The Power of the Velilla Blueberry

In Velilla del Río Carrión, where crystal-clear rivers weave through majestic mountains, deep blue blueberries thrive in pristine conditions. Packed with antioxidants and natural purity, these superfruits have fueled local adventurers for centuries. Today, they form an essential component of our energy drink, enhancing its appeal as one of the finest sports beverages and a top isotonic drink on the market.

The Refreshment of the Sierra de Francia Cherry

In the heart of the Sierra de Francia in Salamanca, cherry trees burst into vibrant red, symbolising vitality and natural refreshment. These cherries, celebrated over generations for their ability to naturally rejuvenate and invigorate, infuse our drink with a unique burst of flavour. This not only elevates its taste but also supports its function as an electrolyte drink and an effective hangover cure for those long nights.

The Natural Strength of Bierzo Honey

Harvested from the rolling hills of Bierzo, golden honey is a natural powerhouse of energy. Used for centuries as a source of strength and recovery, its rich sweetness enhances flavour while providing the sustained boost needed for those who push their limits. This ingredient is pivotal in our energy drink, cementing its status as a premium isotonic drink and a reliable sports beverage.

A Drink That Blends Nature with Energy

By harnessing the extraordinary properties of these three exceptional ingredients, our drink is expertly crafted to hydrate, revitalize, and support an active lifestyle. With its unique formula, it stands out not only as an energy drink but also as a refreshing sports beverage and electrolyte drink, with added benefits as a hangover cure. Whether you're recovering after a night out or gearing up for a demanding day, our drink offers the perfect fusion of nature and science to help you conquer every challenge.
